About

Born in 1992 in Punta Alta, Buenos Aires Province, Argentina, into an Argentine-Italian family, I studied Architecture and Urbanism at the National University of La Plata.

During my academic years, I began practicing photography in an intuitive way. After discovering analog photography, and with a continuous interest in architecture, this discipline gradually became a natural extension of my professional practice.

My experience as an architect, both in Argentina and in Paris, together with the travels I have undertaken across different parts of the world over the past years, has allowed me to develop a perspective that combines technical understanding with visual sensitivity.
