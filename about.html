About

Architect and architectural photographer.

Born in 1992 in Argentina, I studied at the Faculty of Architecture and Urbanism at the National University of La Plata. During my university years, I began practicing photography. After discovering analog photography, and with a continued interest in architecture, this discipline gradually became a natural extension of my professional practice.

My experience as an architect, both in Argentina and in Paris, along with my travels to different parts of the world in recent years, has allowed me to develop a perspective that combines technical knowledge and visual sensitivity.
